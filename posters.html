Poster for the Drug and Alcohol Rehabilitation Center “Chance”

Year:2018

Involvement: Fonts research,creation of all visual assets including logo and photo-materials.

For this poster I decided to use strong visual message of a hand (by the way it’s mine) which is holding a tied to it with chains bottle of alcohol as a representation of alcohol addiction. In the upper right part I created text composition which consists of the logo, tag-line of the Rehabilitation Center: “Professional treatment and rehabilitation of drug adiction, alcoholism and gambling addiction”. Tag-line of the poster says “Rehabilitation center “Chance”. The key from your addiction!” - as it resonates with the lock in the bottom part of the poster. I used red color as an accent for the logo and tag-line. Overall, color palette of the poster is dark and disturbing as the representation of this problem in modern society.

Portrait stylization poster

Year:2016

Involvement: Creation of the gift poster

I did this piece in the far 2016 but it actually one of my favourites. Client wanted to create a gift-poster for his friend in “GTA San-Andreas” game loading screen style. I tried my best working with gradients, strokes and colours to recreate it. The biggest hardship of this piece was to repeat the style of original font composition and to make background expressive to represent provincial Ukranian city atmosphere and at the same time not to outweigh the main Illustration.
